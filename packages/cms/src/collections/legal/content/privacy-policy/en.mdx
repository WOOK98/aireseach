## Who We Are

Airesearch is operated by its individual developer ("Wook"). Contact: hello@airesearchs.com.

This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use airesearchs.com, create an account, use research and report-generation features, or contact us.

## Information We Collect

We may collect account information such as your email address, name, authentication details, organization membership, preferences, and communications with us.

We may collect usage information such as pages visited, actions taken, device and browser details, approximate location derived from network information, timestamps, logs, diagnostics, and security events.

If you use paid features, payment details are handled by a payment processor. We may receive limited payment-related records such as plan, subscription status, transaction identifiers, billing email, amount, currency, tax information, and payment status. We do not intend to store full payment card numbers.

If you provide API keys or similar credentials, we use them to perform the actions you request, validate access, route requests, measure usage, troubleshoot failures, and protect the service. You are responsible for keeping your API keys authorized and current.

If you generate reports or use research tools, we may process the inputs you submit, selected tickers or entities, uploaded or pasted material, prompts, generated report content, feedback, and related metadata. Report generation content may include financial, market, company, industry, supply-chain, or other decision-support material that you provide or request.

## How We Use Information

We use information to provide, secure, maintain, personalize, and improve Airesearch; create and manage accounts; deliver report-generation and research workflows; process subscriptions and invoices; respond to support requests; prevent fraud and abuse; debug service issues; enforce our terms; and comply with applicable obligations.

We may use aggregated or de-identified information to understand product performance, improve research quality, and develop new features. We do not use your account email data, API keys, or private report-generation content to identify you in public materials.

## Sharing Information

We may share information with service partners that help us operate Airesearch, such as hosting, analytics, authentication, email delivery, payment processing, storage, monitoring, security, and support tools. These parties may process information only as needed to provide services to us.

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, to investigate abuse, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or transfer of assets involving Airesearch.

## Report Content and Financial Information

Airesearch produces research outputs for decision support. Reports, summaries, scores, forecasts, rankings, and assistant responses are not investment, legal, tax, accounting, or other professional advice. You are responsible for independently verifying information before making decisions.

You should avoid submitting sensitive personal information, regulated data, confidential third-party information, or API keys inside prompts or report content unless it is necessary for your intended use and you have the right to provide it.

## Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide Airesearch, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, maintain security, and support legitimate business needs. Retention periods may vary by data type and feature. Account records are retained while your account is active and are deleted or de-identified within a reasonable period after account closure, except where longer retention is required by law.

## Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials and API keys.

## Your Choices

You may update account information, request deletion, manage communications, revoke or rotate API keys, and stop using paid features subject to the terms that apply to your plan.

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, export, or object to certain processing of your personal information. To make a request, contact hello@airesearchs.com.

## International Processing

Information may be processed in locations where Airesearch or its service partners operate. Airesearch is hosted on infrastructure located primarily in the United States.

## Children

Airesearch is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law. Minimum age: 18.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date or notice.
